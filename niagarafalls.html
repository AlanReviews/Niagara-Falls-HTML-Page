Travel to Niagara Falls

Niagara Falls is a waterfall located in the Canada-United States border. It provides electricity for its citizens. It is a very crowded place. The weather is nice during the summer and very cold during the Winter.

Why Go there?

  • Experience the beauty of the falls
  • Learn about the creation of the falls
  • An amazing boat/canoe/kayak ride!
  • Watch it light up at night

Where is it?

In between Ontario and New York. In order to get there, type this in your GPS: Niagara Falls. If you do not live in North America, you can fly there.

What to Eat

You can eat many types of food. People can eat pasta, fish, vegetables, deserts, and more. You can find food at restaurants. Buying at convinient stores is expensive. The food is healthy and high quality.

For drinks, people can drink water, juice, and coffee.
